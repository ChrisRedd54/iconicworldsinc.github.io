Overview

Iconic Worlds Inc. acquires, develops, and monetizes brand, trademark, likeness, and character intellectual property.

Business Model

The company operates a rights-based, yield-driven licensing model spanning media & entertainment licensing, lifestyle & apparel, consumer products, experiential activations, gaming & interactive, music & performance rights, and digital & social licensing. IW maintains centralized governance, standardized contract administration, quarterly audit enforcement, and multi-vertical rights management designed for predictable royalty yield.
