Overview

Iconic Worlds Inc. acquires and licenses trademarks, brands, likeness, and character intellectual property through contract-secured, recurring royalty agreements.

Business Model

The company operates a rights-based, yield-driven licensing model across media & entertainment licensing, lifestyle & apparel, consumer products & merchandise, experiential activations, gaming & interactive, music & performance rights, and digital & social licensing. Iconic Worlds Inc. maintains centralized governance, standardized contract administration, quarterly audit enforcement, and multi-vertical rights management engineered for predictable royalty yield.
